Estimate Book Weight:
1. A book weights about one to two pounds. If you have a lot of books, please get a shipping scale.

2. If you don't have a scale, you can estimate by the number of pages in the book and the size of the book:
A regular sized book (size about 6x8 inches), weights 1 oz for every 12.5 sheets. Count the number of sheets in a book to estimate the weight.
a. For pages with number on it, find number on the last page, divide that number by two.
b. For pages without number on it, count the number of sheets.
Add up a and b, and you have the total number of sheets.

3. Formula: Weight = ([number of sheets without page number]+[number of pages]/2 )/12.5 (oz) If your book is ony half the size of the regular sized book, say size 4x6 inches, then divide above result by two again.
